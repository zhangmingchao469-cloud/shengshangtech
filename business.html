Business Services

We deliver full-stack mobile app and game services through three proven business modes, each designed to match your product stage and market ambition.

1. Project Operations Agency

For teams with existing products that need better growth, retention, and commercial performance in global markets.

  • Growth strategy and KPI system setup
  • Product event planning and content cadence
  • User lifecycle and retention operation
  • Cross-region campaign adaptation and analytics

2. Project R&D Agency

For partners seeking experienced engineering execution from architecture and coding to launch delivery.

  • Product architecture and technical feasibility
  • Cross-platform mobile app/game development
  • Performance optimization and release quality control
  • Store submission package and launch support

3. Self-developed Project Operation

We build and run in-house products, continuously improving user value and monetization efficiency with long-term operation.

  • Product ideation and global opportunity discovery
  • Data-driven version iteration
  • Global channel operation and compliance alignment
  • Commercial strategy optimization

Monetization Models in Self-developed Projects

  • Auto-renewable and period-based in-app subscriptions
  • Virtual items, packages, and progression-based purchases
  • Premium feature unlocking and advanced toolkits
  • Tiered VIP memberships and loyalty privileges
  • Compliant ad monetization with region-aware controls

Operational Principles

  • User value first, long-term product sustainability second, short-term revenue third.
  • Regionalized operation policy to respect local culture and regulation.
  • Continuous compliance checks for stores, privacy, and monetization channels.
  • Transparent reporting and milestone-based delivery for all collaboration models.
